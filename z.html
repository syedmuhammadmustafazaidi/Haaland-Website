‘Imagine beating Erling Haaland!’ - Cristiano Ronaldo ‘proud’ of besting Man City ‘animal’ in 2023 goalscoring charts as Al-Nassr star jokes he’ll retire ‘in 10 years’

About Today..

"Haaland is an exceptional talent with a bright future ahead. His skills and determination on the field are truly remarkable. It's a pleasure to watch him play and I wish him all the best in his career." - Cristiano Ronaldo

Ronaldo claimed the former after netting 54 goals in 59 games for Al-Nassr and Portugal. Haaland notched 50 in 60 games for Manchester City and for the Norway national side. After collecting his latest award, Ronaldo took to the stage. During his speech, he talked up Haaland’s talents. “I was the best goalscorer this season,” the now Al-Nassr forward said. “Imagine beating young animals like Haaland… I’m proud. And I’ll be 39 soon! I like when people doubt me again and then I’m successful. I don’t get affected by the criticism.”
